# Rusty Gun Pricing Strategy

## Business Model Overview

Rusty Gun follows the **open core** business model with AGPL v3 licensing:

- **Core software**: Free and open source under AGPL v3
- **Revenue streams**: Commercial support, hosted services, and enterprise features
- **Community benefit**: All improvements remain open source

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Commercial Support & Services
**Target**: Enterprise customers, large deployments

**Services**:
- Professional support (24/7, 4-hour response)
- Custom development and integrations
- Training and consulting
- SLA guarantees (99.9% uptime)
- On-site support and implementation

**Pricing**:
- **Starter**: $2,000/month (up to 10 developers)
- **Professional**: $5,000/month (up to 50 developers)
- **Enterprise**: $15,000/month (unlimited developers)
- **Custom**: Contact sales for large deployments

### 2. Hosted Services (SaaS)
**Target**: Teams and companies wanting managed Rusty Gun

**Features**:
- Fully managed Rusty Gun instances
- Automatic scaling and backups
- Monitoring and alerting
- Security updates and maintenance
- 99.9% uptime SLA

**Pricing**:
- **Developer**: $29/month (1 instance, 1GB storage)
- **Team**: $99/month (5 instances, 10GB storage)
- **Business**: $299/month (20 instances, 100GB storage)
- **Enterprise**: $999/month (unlimited instances, 1TB storage)

### 3. Enterprise Features
**Target**: Large organizations with specific needs

**Features**:
- Advanced security and compliance tools
- Enterprise SSO integration
- Custom authentication providers
- Advanced monitoring and analytics
- White-label solutions
- Custom integrations and APIs

**Pricing**:
- **Enterprise License**: $10,000/year per organization
- **Custom Development**: $200/hour
- **White-label**: $50,000 one-time + $5,000/month

### 4. Training & Certification
**Target**: Developers and teams learning Rusty Gun

**Offerings**:
- Online courses and tutorials
- Certification programs
- Workshops and bootcamps
- Custom training for teams

**Pricing**:
- **Individual Course**: $99
- **Certification Program**: $299
- **Team Training**: $2,000/day
- **Custom Workshop**: $5,000/day

## Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based Pricing
- Price based on value delivered, not cost
- Enterprise customers pay more for support and reliability
- Open source users get full functionality for free

### Freemium Model
- Core software is completely free
- Premium features and services are paid
- Clear upgrade path from free to paid

### Community First
- All core improvements remain open source
- Community contributions are welcomed and valued
- Revenue funds continued development

## Competitive Analysis

### vs. MongoDB
- **MongoDB**: Complex licensing, expensive enterprise features
- **Rusty Gun**: Simple AGPL, transparent pricing, better developer experience

### vs. Redis
- **Redis**: Dual licensing, expensive enterprise features
- **Rusty Gun**: Single license, more affordable, better local-first features

### vs. Elasticsearch
- **Elasticsearch**: Complex licensing, expensive
- **Rusty Gun**: Simple licensing, more affordable, better developer experience

## Conclusion

The AGPL v3 + open core model provides:
- Strong community adoption through free core
- Clear revenue streams through services
- Protection against proprietary forks
- Sustainable business model

This approach balances open source values with business sustainability, ensuring Rusty Gun can thrive while remaining true to its open source roots.
